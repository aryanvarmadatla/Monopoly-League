Monopoly Rating Score (MRS)
The number used to rank players — not raw points
MRS = (adjustedWinRate × 100) + (points × 0.5) + (log(games + 1) × 2) + (soloWins × 0.3)
  • Adjusted win rate — uses a Bayesian smoothing formula that adds ~5 phantom "average" games to your record. This prevents a player with 1 win from 1 game from claiming a 100% win rate above someone with 8 wins from 10 games.
  • Points factor — raw points still matter as a secondary signal, rewarding players who consistently score high finishes.
  • Volume bonus — playing more games helps your MRS, but with logarithmic diminishing returns. Playing 50 games doesn't dominate over someone with 15.
  • Solo win bonus — a small extra reward for each solo win, since they're harder to achieve than team wins.
⚠️
Provisional Players
Fewer than 5 games played
  • Players with fewer than 5 games are marked Provisional in the table.
  • They are ranked by the same MRS formula — the Bayesian adjustment naturally keeps outlier win rates in check.
  • The badge disappears automatically once 5 games are logged.
📉
Relegation Zone
Applies when 4+ players are in the league
  • The bottom 2 players are highlighted in red — this is the relegation zone.
  • It's a visual warning only, not a hard penalty — use it to motivate a comeback.
  • Relegation zone only activates with 4 or more players in the league.
📝
Logging a Game
How to record results correctly
  • Solo games: Select all players who played, then assign finishing positions in bankruptcy order — 1st is the winner (last standing), last is the first person eliminated.
  • Team games: Choose the number of teams and players per team, assign players to each team, then rank the teams by finishing order. All teammates share the same points.
  • Everyone must be ranked. There are no "also played" participants — if you played, you get a finishing position.
  • Games are logged with today's date automatically. Mistakes can be deleted and moved to the Deleted games tab where they can be reviewed or restored.
Rules are maintained by the league admin. If you think something needs changing, reach out — don't edit game logs to work around the system.
