Monopoly League — Official Rules

How points are earned, how the table is ranked, and what keeps everything fair. Updated whenever the league admin pushes a change.

📊 How Rankings Work
📈
Primary sort: Median Points Per Game (Med PPG)
Your typical game — not inflated by outliers
1st: Median PPG — the middle value of all your per-game scores. Immune to one huge blowout game inflating your average.
2nd: Avg PPG — mean points per game. Tiebreaker when medians are equal.
3rd: Win % — percentage of games where you finished 1st. Tiebreaker when both PPG values are equal.
4th: Total points — raw accumulated points. Rewards players who play more at equal efficiency.
5th: Name (alphabetical) — last resort tiebreaker.
  • Why median, not average? A single massive game (e.g. winning a 6-player solo) can inflate a mean average and make a player look better than they really are. Median shows your consistent, typical performance.
  • Why not total points? Someone who plays 30 games will almost always have more total points than someone with 10 games — even if the 10-game player is clearly better. PPG removes that bias. MLS used PPG for exactly this reason during their 2020 season when teams played different numbers of games.
  • Provisional players (fewer than 10 games) are ranked below all established players regardless of their Med PPG. A sample of under 10 games isn't stable enough to rank fairly.

⚖️
Team vs Solo comparison
How they stack up
  • Solo 1st (4–8 pts) is always more than or equal to team 1st (3–4 pts) at every field size, since you're winning alone.
  • 2-team games are win-or-nothing — 2nd place gets 0 because there's no meaningful "middle" finish with only two teams.
  • 3-team (2v2v2) rewards 2nd place because beating one of three teams is a real accomplishment worth acknowledging.
  • 3v3 rewards more per player than 2v2 since coordinating 3 people is harder.
⚙️ Other Rules
⚠️
Provisional Players
Fewer than 10 games played
  • Marked Provisional in the table.
  • Ranked below all established players regardless of stats — fewer than 10 games is not a reliable sample.
  • Badge disappears automatically once 10 games are logged.
📉
Relegation Zone
Bottom 2 when 4+ players in league
  • Bottom 2 players highlighted in red when the league has 4 or more established players.
  • Visual warning only — no automatic penalty.
  • Use it as motivation to climb back up.
📝
Logging Games
How to record results correctly
  • Solo games: Select all players, then assign finishing positions in order — 1st is the last player standing, last is the first eliminated.
  • Team games: Choose the format (2v2, 3v3, 2v2v2), assign players to teams, then rank the teams. All teammates share the same points per the table above.
  • Everyone must be ranked. No unranked participants — if you played, you get a position.
  • Mistakes? Delete the game log — it moves to the Deleted games tab where anyone can view it and restore it if needed.
Rules are maintained by the league admin. If you think something should change, reach out — don't manipulate game logs to work around the system.
